DATA ANALYSIS ON REVENUE ALLOCATION IN NIGERIA

1/3
2/3
3/3
❮ ❯

This project analyses the summary of distribution of revenue allocation to Local Government Councils in Nigeria by Federation Account Allocation Committee for the month of November, 2021 Shared in December, 2021. The project also contains a well designed dashboard. The aim of this project was to utilize my analytic skills in other areas apart from science and engineering. The analysis showed that the northern part of the country received more allocation in the month of november than the southern part. This was an impressive project for me.
