Variable coefficients differential equation calculator

1/3
2/3
3/3
❮ ❯

A smart variable coefficient differential equation calculator that evaluates the general and particular solutions to the differential equation below: y" + Axy' - nAy = 0 where n represents an integer while A represents any number(including negative numbers). The methodology used for this calculator was developed by Innocent Chukwuma Obasi in his publication 'New solutions to variable coefficients differential equation (2021, ongoing review)'. This method proves to be better than other contemporary methods. This calculator is able to detect invalid inputs for correction, and also ensure a readable and understandable output.
